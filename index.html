Co-Owner & Rider

ROB BARBER

Rob Barber is the co-owner, rider and coach of PRB Racing — a racer who needs no introduction. Racing since 1998, Rob has competed at the highest levels of the sport: club racing, BSB, WSB, the Isle of Man TT, and National & International Irish Road Races, through to the Pikes Peak International Hill Climb.

Rob made history as the winner of the inaugural Isle of Man TTxGP, and went on to win the Electric class at Pikes Peak — making him the only person in the world to have won races at both events. A true pioneer of electric motorcycle racing.

Co-Owner & Mechanic

PHIL BARBER

Phil Barber is the co-owner and chief mechanic of PRB Racing — and Rob's dad. It was Phil who handed Rob his first bike at just five years old, sparking a passion that would take him to the top of the sport.

A constant presence in the paddock, Phil has been Rob's trusted mechanic throughout his career — from early club days right through to the Isle of Man TT and beyond. Beyond PRB Racing, Phil has also helped coach numerous young talented riders through his Streetstock race bikes, giving the next generation their own start in the sport.

PRB Racing Academy

ERICA LAWSON

After taking the bold step into motorcycle sport in 2024, Erica has progressed rapidly in both race craft and rider confidence. She competes on a regular basis in the Preston & District Motorcycle Club Championship and the ACU Ultra Lightweight Championship, gathering vital experience with every round.

2026 will see Erica return to those championships with greater experience and knowledge at her disposal — with ambitions to push for podium finishes and ultimately compete at the pinnacle of UK bikesport: The British Superbike Championship.

"My goal is to develop as a competitive rider while proudly representing the brands and businesses that support me."
